GSL doesn't make these inverters.晟格锐 · 转售 · 非生产商

A Shenzhen-based battery company resells Deye, Megarevo, and Lux Power inverters under its own sticker. The sales pages don't say so. The forum receipts do. And on November 15, 2024 the Deye-platform half of that catalog got remotely bricked across the US, UK, and Pakistan. This brief is the field-level read before Solarpunk Solutions touches a purchase order.

01 · OEM Map
GSL is a sticker, not a fab.
Founder confirmed the 12 kW unit is a Lux Power LXP-LB-US-12k twin on the DIY Solar Forum. Other SKUs ship as Deye or Megarevo hardware — sometimes without disclosure.
02 · Brick Event
Nov 15 2024 · Deye remote-disable.
Non-Sol-Ark-branded Deye inverters bricked in US/UK/PK with the message "This inverter is not allowed use at Pakistan/USA/UK." GSL's Deye-platform SKUs are exactly the gray-channel hardware that got hit.

Marketing copy, not infrastructure.
Field RMAs route to direct-China email + WeChat/WhatsApp escalation. No NA dealer mesh comparable to EG4 (Signature Solar) or Sol-Ark. One owner paid $600 shipping via the Alibaba supplier.

The lockout, on a timeline.

02 · Brick event chronology

The Sol-Ark / Deye "trade war" Vance flagged is real and well-documented. It came to a head on November 15, 2024 when Deye pushed a firmware update that disabled all non-Sol-Ark-branded Deye inverters in three target markets simultaneously.

2018
Sol-Ark · Deye exclusivity
Sol-Ark signs exclusive US distribution with Deye-made hardware. Other importers begin selling Deye-platform clones under house brands (GSL among them).
2018 → 2024
Gray-channel growth
Chinese factories ship Deye-derived inverters to the US under GSL, Mango, EG4 (some SKUs), Megarevo, and others. Quality is "fine" while it works; firmware OTA pipe stays with the original OEM.
Nov 15 · 2024
Remote disable fires
Deye pushes a firmware update. Non-Sol-Ark-branded Deye inverters in US / UK / Pakistan show: "This inverter is not allowed use at Pakistan/USA/UK." Field installers lose customer systems overnight.
Nov 17 · 2024
Coverage breaks
Solarboi, Hacker News, and Born City independently document the event. Deye/Sol-Ark blame each other. No path to unbrick.
2025 → now
Persistent risk
No public commitment from Deye that this won't happen again. Any gray-channel Deye-platform unit — including the relevant GSL GES & SPI SKUs — remains one firmware push from disable.

What to bring in instead.

04 · Capacity-tier alternatives

For each capacity tier in GSL's lineup, there's a cleaner option — real install base, real US dealer mesh, real RMA pipeline. No undisclosed rebrand, no remote-disable exposure.

3.6 – 6.5 kW · small off-grid
Victron MultiPlus-II
or EG4 6000XP
Victron is the premium option — mature firmware, decade-plus install base, every component documented. EG4 6000XP is the budget play with Signature Solar's US support layer.
8 – 10 kW · split-phase
EG4 12000XP
or Sol-Ark 12K
EG4 has Signature Solar's US-stocked parts depot. Sol-Ark has the full dealer mesh. Either gets you AFCI / RSD / split-phase out of the box with documented firmware.
12 kW · AIO with AFCI/RSD/SmartLoad
EG4 18kPV or Sol-Ark 15K
or Lux LXP-LB-US-12k
The Lux is the actual hardware GSL would sell you as the GES48120-XP — same silicon, real US warranty, no rebrand opacity. EG4 / Sol-Ark are the well-trodden paths.
15 kW · 3-phase commercial
Sol-Ark 30K-3P
or SolarEdge 3ϕ
UL1741-SB current. Proven on commercial sites with real demand-charge profiles. Don't gray-channel three-phase commercial — the AHJ will not be friendly to undocumented OEMs.
